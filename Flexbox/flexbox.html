ARE COLORS JUST A PIGMENT OF OUR IMAGINATION?

lets talk color.

Color, is a property of light as seen by people.

The world is full of light. Visible light is made of seven wavelength groups. These are the colors you see in a rainbow: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.

The reddish colors are the long wavelengths. The greenish colors are the mid-size wavelengths. The bluish color are the short wavelengths.

Black is the absence of color and is therefore not a color.

While there are some colors too complex for us to see, approximately seven million different colors can be seen by the human eye.

Every person sees color differently.

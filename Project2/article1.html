Paper Lanterns

Paper Lanterns is a pop punk/alt rock band from Jackson, Michigan. It was originally a project of lead singer and guitarist Colton Choate that started around 2012 and was revamped when drummer Rocky Hutchins joined in the winter of 2016. After playing as a two-piece and with multiple temporary members for a year, Colton recruited a past bandmate, Dylan May, to play bass.

Since Dylan joined in December of 2017, Paper Lanterns has been playing shows all over Michigan from their hometown of Jackson to Lansing, Grand Rapids, Detroit and more. They released their debut EP, “New Faces”, in March of 2019 on all streaming platforms.

Paper Lanterns’ sound blends nostalgic and modern pop punk with a taste of classic rock. They pride themselves on putting on an engaging, high-energy show at every venue whether they’re playing for 200 people or 20. They hope to get out on the road this summer and play shows around the Midwest, as well as write and record more music.

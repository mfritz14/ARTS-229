Paper Lanterns

Paper Lanterns is a pop punk/alt rock band from Jackson, Michigan. It was originally a project of lead singer and guitarist Colton Choate that started around 2012 and was revamped when drummer Rocky Hutchins joined in the winter of 2016. After playing as a two-piece and with multiple temporary members for a year, Colton recruited a past bandmate, Dylan May, to play bass.
